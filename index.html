Kathleen's Victoria Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 9oz cream flour
  • 6 free range eggs
  • 12oz caster sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • squeeze of an orange
  • a little milk
Filling

strawberry Jam, whipped fresh dairy cream, you can also put what you like in with the jam, apple, kiwi or a few strawberries from the garden.

Oven Temperature 190ºC (375ºF, gas mark 5)
Oven Time 15 - 18 minutes
Method

Line two round tins with greased baking parchment. Use an electric mixer to whisk together the eggs, vanilla and caster sugar for about 12 minutes until the mixture is pale in colour, has doubled in size and can hold a figure of eight made with the whisk. Next, carefully fold in the sieved flour, to the mixture. Add the squeeze of a fresh orange, a little milk can also, be added if the mixture is too stiff. Divide the mixture between the two tins and put into the preheated oven for about 15 – 18 minutes. Remove from the oven and leave to cool on a wire rack. When cool remove the baking parchment from the cake and spread strawberry jam over the top of one of the sponge cakes and layer cream on top of the jam, then place the other sponge on top. Kathleen says if you want to be fancy a little icing sugar can be sieved on top of the cake.
